Pricing FAQs

Do I have to sign a long-term contract?
Nope. Gusto is month-to-month. Cancel anytime.
How do you bill for contractors?
Contractors are billed at the per person cost for any plan you select. We know you might not pay your contractors on a regular schedule, so we only bill you for contractors in months when you’ve paid them.
